Schedule

This course will take a total of 9 weeks, starting on Sunday, July 14th, ending on September 1st. Each class will take about an hour. Note that the dates may change from week to week, so please check the Google Classroom and Slack for details. If you cannot attend a class, there will be recordings posted on the Google Classroom. 

Week 1: Introduction to Algebra (Part 1)

Week 2: Introduction to Algebra (Part 2)

Week 3: Introduction to Counting and Probability (Part 1)

Week 4: Introduction to Counting and Probability (Part 2)

Week 5: Introduction to Number Theory (Part 1)

Week 6: Introduction to Number Theory (Part 2)

Week 7: Introduction to Geometry (Part 1)

Week 8: Introduction to Geometry (Part 2)

Week 9: Review
