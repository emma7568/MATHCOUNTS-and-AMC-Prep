Homework

Now, we must talk about the workload expected from you. There will be homework, but do not worry! It is not mandatory, but we recommend you to try it out. You will only learn if you try the questions yourself. The homework is due until the end of the topic, so you have two weeks to work on twenty questions. If help is ever needed, message either Emma Yu (emmayu7568@gmail.com) or Ella Yu (ellayu4102@gmail.com) for help, and they will assist you the best they can. 

How can I turn my homework in?

We're glad you asked! Just take a picture of your work, and attach it to the assignment on google classroom. We will show you how to do this on the first day.
